# Security Constraints

## Identity & Access (authN/authZ)
- OIDC with RBAC.

## Data Protection
- TLS in transit; AES-256 at rest.

## Secrets Management
- Vault/KMS; no secrets in code.

## Logging & Audit
- Structured logs; tamper-evident audit trail.

## Compliance
- Map to organizational standards; add controls checklist.
